Types of bikes

  • Road bicycle

    • The term road bicycle is used to describe bicycles built for traveling at speed on paved roads. Some sources use the term to mean racing bicycle.

  • Gravel bicycle

    • Our performance gravel bikes are designed to be the fastest! We combined gravel-specific geometry with aerodynamic tubing and ample tire clearance.

  • Cargo bike

    • The Cargo Bike Company designs and builds fully customised cargo bikes and trikes that specialise in moving people and 'stuff'.

  • Racing bicycle

    • The best road racing bikes are reactive and fast handling they should be nimble and agile. This is ideal for racing purposes

  • Touring bicycle

    • Touring bikes are designed to be durable. Touring bikes are commonly steel framed with a better gear ratio

  • Utility bicycle

    • Utility bicycles are principally used for short-distance commuting, running errands, shopping, leisure or for transporting goods or merchandise

  • Mountain bike

    • Mountain bikes are generally specialized for use on mountain trails, single track, fire roads, and other unpaved surfaces.

  • BMX bike

    • BMX bikes are designed for either urban riding or pulling off stunts and tricks on the flat or on ramps at the skate park. Here's our guide to BMX bikes.
